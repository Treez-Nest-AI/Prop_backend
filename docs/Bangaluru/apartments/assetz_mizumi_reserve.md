# Assetz Mizumi Reserve

## Description

Assetz Mizumi Reserve is a premium residential apartment project located near Bagalur in North Bangalore. Developed by Assetz Property Group, the project emphasizes eco-conscious living with thoughtfully planned green landscapes and contemporary architecture.
The homes are designed to offer spacious interiors, functional layouts, and enhanced natural ventilation, creating a comfortable and sustainable living environment for modern families.

## Overview

Strategically positioned in the rapidly growing North Bangalore corridor, Assetz Mizumi Reserve benefits from proximity to Kempegowda International Airport, Aerospace Park, and upcoming infrastructure developments around Bagalur.
The project offers well-planned 3 BHK residences complemented by lifestyle amenities, landscaped open areas, and community-focused design, making it suitable for both end-users and long-term investors.
