# Birla Evara

## Description
Birla Evara is a premium residential apartment development located on Sarjapur Road in East Bangalore. Developed by Birla Estates, the project offers spacious 2, 3, and 4 BHK homes designed with contemporary architecture, elegant interiors, and high-quality finishes.
The residences are thoughtfully planned to ensure maximum natural light, cross ventilation, and efficient space utilization, catering to modern urban families.

## Overview
Strategically positioned in the fast-growing Sarjapur Road corridor, Birla Evara enjoys excellent connectivity to Electronic City, Outer Ring Road (ORR), and Whitefield. The location provides seamless access to major IT parks, educational institutions, hospitals, and lifestyle destinations.
The project integrates landscaped open spaces with modern amenities to create a balanced and comfortable living environment in East Bangalore.
