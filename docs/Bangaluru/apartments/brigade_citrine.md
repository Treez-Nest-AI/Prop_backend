# Brigade Citrine

## Description
Brigade Citrine is a premium residential apartment project located on Old Madras Road, Whitefield, Bangalore. Designed as India’s first Net Zero residential community, the project offers thoughtfully planned 3 and 4 BHK homes that blend modern architecture with sustainable, eco-conscious living.

## Overview
Brigade Citrine is a high-rise residential community spread across approximately 4.3 acres with over 80% open green spaces and a low-density layout of 420+ exclusive homes. Developed as a futuristic, eco-themed township, the project emphasizes energy efficiency, natural ventilation, and responsible living. Its strategic location near Whitefield ensures excellent connectivity to IT hubs, metro stations, schools, hospitals, and major commercial zones in East Bangalore.
