# Century Regalia

## Description

Century Regalia is a luxury residential apartment development located in Indiranagar, one of Bangalore’s most premium and well-established residential neighbourhoods. Developed by Century Real Estate, the project offers expansive 3 and 4 BHK homes featuring contemporary architecture, refined interiors, and high-end finishes.
The residences are designed to deliver spacious layouts, large living areas, and premium specifications suited for upscale urban living.

## Overview

Strategically positioned in Central Bangalore, Century Regalia enjoys excellent connectivity to major commercial districts, business hubs, upscale retail destinations, and lifestyle centres.
Indiranagar’s established social infrastructure, vibrant community atmosphere, and strong capital value appreciation make this project highly attractive for luxury homebuyers seeking exclusivity and convenience.
