# Century Renata

## Description

Century Renata is a luxury residential apartment project located on Richmond Road in Central Bangalore. Developed by Century Real Estate, the project offers premium 3 and 4 BHK residences featuring elegant architecture, expansive interiors, and high-end specifications.
The homes are designed to provide an upscale urban lifestyle with spacious layouts, refined finishes, and a strong emphasis on privacy and exclusivity.

## Overview

Situated in one of Bangalore’s most prestigious residential corridors, Century Renata enjoys excellent connectivity to MG Road, UB City, Richmond Town, and major commercial hubs in Central Bangalore.
The project is designed as an exclusive residential enclave with landscaped surroundings, premium amenities, and ready-to-move-in convenience — making it ideal for buyers seeking immediate possession in a prime location.
