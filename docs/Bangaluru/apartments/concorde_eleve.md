# Concorde Eleve

## Description

Concorde Eleve is a modern residential apartment project located in Electronic City, South Bangalore. Developed by Concorde Group, the project offers thoughtfully designed 2 and 3 BHK homes featuring contemporary architecture and efficient space planning.
The residences are designed to maximise natural light, ventilation, and functional living areas, making them suitable for working professionals and families.

## Overview

Strategically situated in Electronic City, Concorde Eleve benefits from proximity to major IT companies, tech parks, and commercial establishments. The project is well-connected to Hosur Road, NICE Road, and other key parts of South Bangalore.
With landscaped open spaces and lifestyle amenities, the development aims to provide a balanced urban living experience in one of Bangalore’s prominent IT corridors.
