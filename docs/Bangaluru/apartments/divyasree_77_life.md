# Divyasree 77 Life

## Description

Divyasree 77 Life is a premium residential apartment project located in Yemalur near Old Airport Road, East Bangalore. Developed by Divyasree Developers, the project offers spacious 2 and 3 BHK homes featuring contemporary architecture, elegant interiors, and quality finishes.
The residences are thoughtfully designed to provide ample natural light, ventilation, and efficient layouts, ensuring comfortable urban living.

## Overview

Strategically positioned in East Bangalore, Divyasree 77 Life offers excellent connectivity to Whitefield, Outer Ring Road (ORR), Indiranagar, and major IT parks. Despite its central connectivity, the project provides a relatively peaceful residential setting in the Yemalur locality.
Being ready to move, the development is ideal for buyers seeking immediate possession in a well-established micro-market with strong rental and resale demand.
