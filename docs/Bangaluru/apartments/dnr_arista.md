# DNR Arista

## Description
DNR Arista is a premium residential apartment community located in Bellandur along the Outer Ring Road in East Bangalore. Developed by DNR Group, the project offers spacious 2.5 BHK and 3 BHK homes designed with contemporary architecture and thoughtfully planned interiors.
The apartments are designed to maximise natural light, ventilation, and functional living spaces, providing a comfortable and modern lifestyle for urban families.

## Overview
Situated along the highly sought-after Outer Ring Road corridor, DNR Arista enjoys excellent connectivity to major IT hubs including Ecospace, Ecoworld, and Sarjapur Road.
The project is surrounded by well-developed social infrastructure such as reputed schools, hospitals, shopping malls, and entertainment centres, making it an attractive choice for both homebuyers and investors in East Bangalore.
