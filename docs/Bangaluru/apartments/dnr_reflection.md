# DNR Reflection

## Description
DNR Reflection is a premium residential apartment community located along the Outer Ring Road in Bellandur, East Bangalore. Developed by DNR Group, the project offers spacious 3 BHK and 4 BHK homes designed with contemporary architecture and premium specifications.
The apartments feature expansive layouts, large balconies, and abundant natural light to provide a luxurious and comfortable living experience for modern urban families. 

## Overview
Situated in the prime Outer Ring Road corridor, DNR Reflection offers excellent connectivity to major IT hubs including Ecospace, Ecoworld, and RMZ Ecosystem.
The project is surrounded by well-developed social infrastructure such as reputed schools, hospitals, shopping malls, and entertainment centres, making it a desirable residential location for professionals working in the nearby technology parks.
