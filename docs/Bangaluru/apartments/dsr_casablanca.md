# DSR Casablanca

## Description

DSR Casablanca is a premium luxury villa community located off Hennur Road in North Bangalore. Developed by DSR Infrastructure Pvt Ltd, the project offers expansive 4 BHK independent villas designed with contemporary architecture and high-end specifications.

Each villa features spacious living areas, private gardens, large bedrooms, and premium finishes. Thoughtfully planned interiors, wide windows for natural light, and elegant design elements create a refined and comfortable living environment within a serene residential setting.

## Overview

DSR Casablanca is envisioned as an exclusive gated villa enclave that combines privacy, luxury, and modern lifestyle amenities. The development features landscaped gardens, wide internal roads, and well-planned recreational zones that enhance the overall living experience.
Strategically located near Hennur Road, the project offers excellent connectivity to Hebbal, Manyata Tech Park, Outer Ring Road, and Kempegowda International Airport. Hennur Road has emerged as one of the fastest-growing residential corridors in North Bangalore, attracting both end users and investors.
