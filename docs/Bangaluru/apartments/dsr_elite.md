# DSR Elite


## Description
DSR Elite is a premium residential apartment project located in Whitefield, East Bangalore. Developed by DSR Infrastructure Pvt Ltd, the project offers thoughtfully designed 2 and 3 BHK residences that combine contemporary architecture with functional living spaces.
The apartments feature efficient layouts, spacious balconies, and ample natural ventilation. With modern interiors and well-planned living zones, the homes are designed to provide comfort and practicality for urban families and working professionals.


## Overview
DSR Elite is envisioned as a modern residential community offering landscaped open spaces and lifestyle amenities that enhance everyday living. The project focuses on delivering a peaceful and convenient environment within one of Bangalore’s most vibrant IT corridors.
Strategically located in Whitefield, the development enjoys excellent connectivity to ITPL, EPIP Zone, Outer Ring Road, and major tech parks. The presence of metro connectivity and strong social infrastructure further strengthens the residential and investment appeal of the project.
