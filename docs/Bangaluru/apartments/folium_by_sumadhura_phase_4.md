# Folium by Sumadhura – Phase 4

## Description

Folium by Sumadhura – Phase 4 is a premium resort-style residential apartment project located in Whitefield, East Bangalore. The project offers spacious 3 and 4 BHK homes with elegant layouts, premium finishes, wide balconies, and excellent natural light and ventilation, set within a lush, contemporary community.

## Overview

Spread across 16.5 acres with over 83% open spaces, Folium Phase 4 is envisioned as a refined urban retreat featuring a 7+ acre grand central landscape and a 54,000 sq. ft. branded clubhouse. Designed for elevated living, the project blends modern architecture, wellness-focused amenities, and seamless connectivity to IT hubs, metro stations, schools, and social infrastructure of Whitefield.
