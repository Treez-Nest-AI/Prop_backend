# Godrej Lakeside Orchard


## Description
Godrej Lakeside Orchard is a premium residential apartment community located on Sarjapur Road in East Bangalore. Developed by Godrej Properties, the project offers thoughtfully designed 2 BHK, 3 BHK, and 4 BHK homes surrounded by landscaped greenery and open spaces.
The apartments are crafted with efficient layouts, ample natural light, and modern finishes to provide a comfortable and contemporary living experience for families.

Situated in the fast-growing Sarjapur Road corridor, Godrej Lakeside Orchard enjoys excellent connectivity to major IT hubs such as Electronic City, Outer Ring Road, and Whitefield.
The project is located close to reputed schools, hospitals, shopping malls, and entertainment zones, making it a preferred residential destination for IT professionals and urban families.
