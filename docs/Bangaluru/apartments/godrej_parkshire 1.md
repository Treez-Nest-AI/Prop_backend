# Godrej Parkshire


## Description
Godrej Parkshire is a large gated residential apartment community located in Hoskote / Sarakariguttahalli, East Bangalore. Developed by Godrej Properties, the project offers well-planned 2 and 3 BHK residences designed with modern architecture, efficient layouts, and comfortable living spaces.
The homes are crafted to provide ample natural light, ventilation, and functional interiors. With landscaped surroundings and thoughtfully designed amenities, the project aims to create a balanced and family-friendly living environment.


## Overview
Spread across approximately 14 acres, Godrej Parkshire comprises around 5 towers with nearly 1,132 residential units. The development is envisioned as a self-contained residential enclave with open green spaces and lifestyle amenities suited for both end users and investors.
Strategically located in the Hoskote growth corridor, the project offers improving connectivity to key parts of East Bangalore, industrial hubs, and upcoming infrastructure developments, enhancing its long-term residential and investment potential.
