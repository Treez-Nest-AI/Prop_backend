# Hiranandani Torino


## Description
Hiranandani Torino is a premium residential apartment community located within the Hiranandani Gardens township in Hebbal, North Bangalore. Developed by House of Hiranandani, the project offers thoughtfully designed 2 BHK and 3 BHK homes featuring elegant architecture and premium specifications.
The apartments are planned with efficient layouts, ample natural light, and high-quality finishes to provide a comfortable and modern living experience.


## Overview
Situated within the well-planned Hiranandani Gardens township, Hiranandani Torino benefits from integrated infrastructure that includes residential, retail, and lifestyle spaces.
The project enjoys excellent connectivity to major employment hubs such as Manyata Tech Park, Outer Ring Road, and Kempegowda International Airport, making it an ideal location for professionals working in North Bangalore.
