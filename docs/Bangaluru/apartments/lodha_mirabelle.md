# Lodha Mirabelle

## Description

Lodha Mirabelle is a premium luxury residential apartment project located near Manyata Tech Park in Hebbal, North Bangalore. Developed by Lodha Group, the project offers expansive 3, 3.5, and 4 BHK residences designed with contemporary architecture, elegant interiors, and premium specifications.
The homes are thoughtfully planned to maximise space utilisation, natural light, and cross ventilation. Large balconies, refined finishes, and landscaped surroundings create a serene and sophisticated living environment within the city.

## Overview

Lodha Mirabelle is envisioned as an upscale residential enclave offering luxury living with modern amenities and green open spaces. The development blends comfort, connectivity, and exclusivity, catering to discerning homebuyers.
Strategically positioned near Manyata Tech Park, the project enjoys seamless access to Hebbal Flyover, Outer Ring Road, and Kempegowda International Airport. The strong presence of IT parks, commercial centres, and social infrastructure makes it an attractive choice for professionals and families in North Bangalore.
