# Mahindra New Haven


## Description
Mahindra New Haven is a residential apartment community located near Hosur Road in Singasandra, South Bangalore. Developed by Mahindra Lifespaces, the project offers thoughtfully designed 1 BHK and 2 BHK homes aimed at young professionals and small families.
The apartments feature efficient layouts, good natural lighting, and modern finishes to provide comfortable and affordable urban living.


## Overview
Situated along the Hosur Road corridor, Mahindra New Haven enjoys strong connectivity to major employment hubs such as Electronic City and key parts of South Bangalore.
The project benefits from proximity to schools, hospitals, shopping centres, and public transportation, making it a practical residential choice for working professionals.
