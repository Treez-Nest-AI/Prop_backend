# Nikoo Homes

## Description

Nikoo Homes is a premium residential development within the Bhartiya City integrated township in Thanisandra, North Bangalore. Developed by Bhartiya Group, the project offers contemporary 1, 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments designed with modern architecture and thoughtfully planned living spaces.
The homes feature efficient layouts, spacious interiors, and access to a wide range of lifestyle amenities within a vibrant township ecosystem.

## Overview

Located in the rapidly developing Thanisandra corridor, Nikoo Homes benefits from excellent connectivity to Manyata Tech Park, Hebbal, and Kempegowda International Airport.
As part of the larger Bhartiya City township, residents enjoy access to residential towers, retail boulevards, office spaces, hospitality developments, and recreational zones — creating a self-sustained urban community environment.
