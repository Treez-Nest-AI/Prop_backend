# Peninsula Heights

## Description

Peninsula Heights is a premium residential apartment development located in Jakkur, North Bangalore. The project offers well-designed 2 and 3 BHK homes featuring contemporary architecture, spacious interiors, and functional layouts suited for modern urban living.
The apartments are crafted to ensure optimal ventilation, natural lighting, and efficient use of space, making them suitable for both families and working professionals.

## Overview

Situated in the rapidly developing Jakkur micro-market, Peninsula Heights benefits from strong connectivity to Hebbal, Manyata Tech Park, and Kempegowda International Airport. The project enjoys proximity to major IT hubs, reputed schools, hospitals, and retail destinations.
North Bangalore’s ongoing infrastructure growth, including airport corridor expansion and improved road connectivity, enhances the long-term investment potential of the project.
