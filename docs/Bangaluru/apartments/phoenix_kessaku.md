# Phoenix Kessaku


## Description
Phoenix Kessaku is an ultra-luxury residential apartment project located in Rajajinagar, West Bangalore. Developed by Phoenix Mills Ltd, the project offers premium 3 BHK, 4 BHK, and 5 BHK residences inspired by elegant Japanese architectural themes.
The homes are designed with expansive layouts, premium interiors, and large balconies that provide panoramic views of the city skyline, offering residents an exceptional luxury living experience.


## Overview
Situated in the prime locality of Rajajinagar, Phoenix Kessaku enjoys excellent connectivity to Central Bangalore and major commercial hubs. The project is part of the prestigious Phoenix mixed-use development located near Orion Mall and the World Trade Center.
Residents benefit from access to top-tier retail, dining, and entertainment options while enjoying a sophisticated residential environment with landscaped gardens and luxury lifestyle amenities.
