# Prestige Evergreen

## Description

Prestige Evergreen is a large-scale township residential project located on Varthur–Whitefield Road in East Bangalore. Developed by Prestige Group, the project offers a wide range of 1, 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments designed with contemporary architecture and thoughtfully planned layouts.
The homes are crafted to maximise natural light, ventilation, and space efficiency. With modern interiors, spacious balconies, and well-planned living areas, the project aims to provide a premium lifestyle within a vibrant township environment.

## Overview

Spread across approximately 24 acres, Prestige Evergreen is planned with around 14 towers and nearly 2,000 residential units. The development is envisioned as a self-sustained township offering landscaped open spaces, lifestyle amenities, and community-centric planning.
Strategically located in the Whitefield corridor, the project enjoys excellent connectivity to IT parks, business hubs, educational institutions, healthcare centres, and shopping destinations. The area’s strong infrastructure growth and metro connectivity further enhance its residential and investment appeal.
