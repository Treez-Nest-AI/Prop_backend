# Prestige Raintree Park

## Description
Prestige Raintree Park is a luxury residential apartment project in Whitefield overlooking Varthur Lake. Spread across ~21 acres, it offers premium 3, 4, and 5 BHK homes with expansive layouts, elegant interiors, and high-quality finishes.

## Overview
Designed as a nature-inspired luxury community, the project maximises green spaces, lake views, natural light, and ventilation. Its Whitefield location ensures seamless connectivity to IT parks, metro stations, schools, and healthcare facilities.

## Floor Plans
Multiple smartly designed 3, 4, and 5 BHK layouts with spacious living areas and private balconies.

- 4 BHK: 2451 – 2968 sq. ft.
- 5 BHK: 3503 – 3698 sq. ft.
