# Pride Sunrise


## Description
Pride Sunrise is a premium residential apartment community located near Bannerghatta Road in South Bangalore. Developed by Pride Group, the project offers thoughtfully designed 2 BHK and 3 BHK homes featuring modern architecture and functional layouts.
The apartments are crafted with efficient space planning, good ventilation, and contemporary finishes to provide a comfortable urban living experience for families.

Situated in the well-connected Bannerghatta Road corridor, Pride Sunrise enjoys convenient access to major employment hubs such as Electronic City, JP Nagar, and other commercial centres in South Bangalore.
