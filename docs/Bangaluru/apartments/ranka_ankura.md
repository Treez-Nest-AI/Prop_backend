# Ranka Ankura

## Description
Ranka Ankura is a modern residential apartment project on Thanisandra Main Road featuring thoughtfully designed homes with premium amenities and vastu-friendly layouts.

## Overview
The project blends comfort, openness, and everyday convenience with excellent connectivity to major city hubs and social infrastructure.

## Floor Plans
Efficiently planned 2, 3, and 4 BHK layouts offering spacious interiors and functional living zones.

## Unit Details
- 2 BHK: 1285 sq. ft.
- 3 BHK: 1485 – 1685 sq. ft.
- 4 BHK: 2075 sq. ft.

## Amenities
- Multipurpose Hall  
- Gymnasium  
- Yoga Room  
- Virtual Golf  
- Business Lounge  
- 35+ World-Class Amenities  

## Builders
- Ranka Builder: Ranka Group is one of Bengaluru's oldest and most established real estate firms, credited with pioneering the concept of self-sustained apartment complexes in the city as early as the 1970s.

## Pricing
Starting from ₹ 1.3 Cr onwards

## Review
Ranka Ankura stands out for vastu-friendly homes, premium planning, and its strategic Thanisandra location.
