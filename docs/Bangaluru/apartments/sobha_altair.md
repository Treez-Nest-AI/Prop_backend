# Sobha Altair

## Description

Sobha Altair is a premium residential apartment project located on Sarjapur Road in East Bangalore. Developed by Sobha, the project offers spacious 3 and 4 BHK residences designed with high-end specifications and superior construction quality.
The homes feature expansive living spaces, large balconies, premium finishes, and thoughtfully designed layouts that maximise ventilation and natural light. Sobha’s reputation for quality craftsmanship and attention to detail reflects strongly in this development.

## Overview

Sobha Altair is positioned in one of Bangalore’s fastest-growing residential corridors – Sarjapur Road. The project is planned with modern architecture, landscaped open areas, and lifestyle amenities catering to premium homebuyers.
The location offers excellent connectivity to Outer Ring Road (ORR), Electronic City, Whitefield, and major IT hubs. Proximity to reputed schools, hospitals, tech parks, and retail centres makes it an ideal choice for families and professionals.
