# Sobha Magnus

## Description

Sobha Magnus is a premium luxury residential apartment project located on Bannerghatta Road, South Bangalore. Developed by Sobha Limited, the project offers expansive 3 and 4 BHK residences designed with contemporary architecture, elegant interiors, and premium specifications.
The homes are thoughtfully planned with spacious layouts, large windows for natural lighting, and well-designed balconies that enhance ventilation and openness. The development focuses on delivering a refined and comfortable urban lifestyle within a prime residential corridor.

## Overview

Sobha Magnus is envisioned as an upscale residential enclave spread across approximately 5.85 acres with around 5 towers and nearly 294 exclusive units. The project blends modern architecture with landscaped open spaces and high-end lifestyle amenities.
Strategically located on Bannerghatta Road, the project offers strong connectivity to major commercial hubs, educational institutions, hospitals, and entertainment zones in South Bangalore. The corridor’s steady infrastructure growth enhances both residential comfort and long-term investment potential.
