# Sumadhura E-119

## Description

Sumadhura E-119 is a premium low-density residential apartment project located in Tubarahalli, Whitefield, East Bangalore. The project offers elegantly designed 3 and 4 BHK homes with smart home features, vastu-compliant layouts, wide balconies, and excellent natural light and ventilation, set amidst lush tropical landscapes.

## Overview

Spread across an exclusive 11.66-acre enclave with over 80% open spaces, Sumadhura E-119 is envisioned as a refined resort-style urban retreat. The community features a grand 40,000+ sq. ft. clubhouse, serene green zones, and thoughtfully planned residences with seamless connectivity to ORR, Metro stations, major IT hubs, schools, and social infrastructure of Whitefield.
