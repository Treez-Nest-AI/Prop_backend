# Nambiar Ellegenza Phase 2


## Description
Nambiar Ellegenza Phase 2 is an ultra-luxury villa community located at Kaggalipura, off Sarjapur Road, Bangalore. Spread across more than 17 acres, the project offers exclusive 4 BHK villas with private media rooms, combining contemporary architecture, lush landscapes, and premium specifications for refined, future-ready living.

## Overview
Nambiar Ellegenza Phase 2 is a low-density gated villa enclave featuring just 220 handcrafted homes designed for families who value space, privacy, and elegance. With expansive layouts, private gardens, and thoughtfully planned open spaces, the community delivers a resort-inspired lifestyle backed by world-class amenities. Its strategic location ensures seamless connectivity to Electronic City, major IT hubs, reputed schools, hospitals, and retail destinations, supported by Nambiar Builders’ 25+ years of trusted legacy.
