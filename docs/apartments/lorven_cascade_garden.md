# Lorven Cascade Garden

## Description

Lorven Cascade Garden is a premium low-density residential apartment project located in Nagondanahalli, Whitefield, East Bangalore. The project offers spacious 3 BHK homes with large balconies, Vastu-compliant layouts, premium finishes, and excellent natural light and ventilation, designed for refined urban living.

## Overview

Spread across 4.3 acres with 75% open spaces and just 312 exclusive residences, Lorven Cascade Garden is envisioned as a contemporary, peaceful enclave with G+9 elevation and a high UDS of 36% for long-term value. The project features RERA & BBMP approvals, two car parks per apartment, and seamless connectivity to Metro stations, ORR, and major IT hubs, making it an ideal blend of comfort, convenience, and investment potential in East Bangalore.
