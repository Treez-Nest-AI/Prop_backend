# Nambiar District 25 (Phase 2)

## Description
Nambiar District 25 – Phase 2 is an ultra-luxury high-rise residential development located off Sarjapur Road, East Bangalore, within a grand 100-acre integrated township. The project offers spacious 2, 3, 4 & 4.5 BHK residences with only four homes per floor, ensuring maximum privacy, natural light, and cross-ventilation, crafted for refined urban living.

## Overview
Designed as an iconic vertical township with nearly 80% open spaces, Nambiar District 25 blends expansive green landscapes with world-class lifestyle infrastructure. Spread across over 63 acres, the project features a 2.5 lakh sq. ft. grand clubhouse, infinity pool, wellness zones, and multiple sports and leisure amenities, all while offering seamless connectivity to IT hubs, international schools, hospitals, and premium social infrastructure of East Bangalore.

## Floor Plans
Multiple intelligently designed 2, 3, 4 & 4.5 BHK floor plans offering large living areas, private balconies, domestic help rooms (in larger units), and well-defined functional zones.
