# Sumadhura Capitol Residences

## Description

Sumadhura Capitol Residences is a premium low-density residential apartment project located next to ITPL in Whitefield, East Bangalore. The project offers spacious 3 and 4 BHK Vastu-compliant homes with productivity-focused layouts, dedicated work-from-home niches, excellent privacy, and modern specifications.

## Overview

Spread across 4.1 acres with 77% open and green spaces, Sumadhura Capitol Residences is designed as a refined urban enclave with no towers facing each other, ensuring privacy and serene views. The community features contemporary architecture, a modern clubhouse, lush landscaped zones, and seamless connectivity to Kadugodi Metro Station, ORR, leading schools, hospitals, malls, and major IT hubs, making it an ideal blend of luxury, comfort, and long-term value.
