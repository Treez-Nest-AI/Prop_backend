# Trendsquares Akino

## Description

Trendsquares Akino is a premium residential apartment project located in Panathur, Whitefield, East Bangalore. The project offers spacious and elegantly designed 3 & 4 BHK homes with large balconies, home automation, 100% Vastu-compliant East & North facing units, and excellent natural light and ventilation, crafted for refined urban living.

## Overview

Spread across 4.7 acres with 3 contemporary towers and 418 exclusive residences, Trendsquares Akino is envisioned as a low-density, well-planned gated community with a grand 23,000 sq. ft. clubhouse and over 50 lifestyle amenities. With BBMP & RERA approvals, possession by December 2027, and seamless connectivity to Whitefield, Bellandur, Sarjapura, ORR, and major IT hubs, the project offers a perfect blend of luxury, convenience, and long-term investment value in East Bangalore.
