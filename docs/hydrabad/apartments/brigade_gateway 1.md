# Brigade Gateway

## Description

Brigade Gateway is a landmark mixed-use residential development by Brigade Group, located in Kokapet, Hyderabad. Designed for elevated urban living, the project offers premium 3 and 4 BHK apartments along with expansive 5 and 6 BHK duplex homes. As Hyderabad’s first integrated mixed-use destination, Brigade Gateway seamlessly blends iconic architecture, world-class amenities, and exceptional connectivity with long-term investment value.

## Overview
Brigade Gateway is an iconic mixed-use development spread across approximately **9.71 acres**, featuring a soaring **G+59 residential tower** housing **580 exclusive residences**. The project includes thoughtfully designed 3 and 4 BHK apartments and grand 5 and 6 BHK duplex homes with premium specifications and spacious layouts. What sets Brigade Gateway apart is its integration with a **World Trade Center**, **InterContinental 5-star hotel**, and **Orion Mall**, creating a truly global lifestyle destination. Located in Kokapet, the project enjoys excellent connectivity to the Financial District, major IT hubs, schools, healthcare facilities, and retail destinations, making it a prestigious address for luxury living and long-term capital appreciation.
