# Candeur Skyline 

## Description
Candeur Skyline (The Zenith) is an ultra-luxury residential apartment project located in Puppalaguda, Hyderabad’s prestigious Financial District. Rising as South India’s tallest residential landmark with 59 floors, the project offers exclusive 4 BHK sky apartments designed for absolute privacy, expansive living, and unmatched skyline views, complemented by world-class amenities and concierge services.

## Overview
Candeur Skyline (The Zenith) is a landmark ultra-luxury residential development spread across approximately **4.04 acres**, comprising **59 floors** with **one exclusive sky apartment per floor**, totaling **282 ultra-luxury residences**. The project offers meticulously planned homes with expansive layouts ranging from **6,520 to 11,999 sq. ft.**, featuring **13-ft high ceilings**, **200+ sq. ft. balconies**, abundant natural light, and breathtaking panoramic views of the city skyline.
Strategically located in the Financial District, the project enjoys seamless connectivity to **Hitech City, Gachibowli, Kokapet, ORR, and Hyderabad International Airport**, making it a prime choice for elite homeowners and long-term luxury investors.
