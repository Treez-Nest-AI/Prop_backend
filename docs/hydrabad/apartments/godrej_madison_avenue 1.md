# Godrej Madison Avenue

## Description
Godrej Madison Avenue is a Manhattan-inspired luxury residential apartment project by Godrej Properties, located in Kokapet, Hyderabad. Designed to capture the vibrant energy, sophistication, and cultural depth of New York City, the project blends iconic architecture with premium lifestyle amenities to deliver a refined urban living experience. Its strategic location on Golden Mile Road ensures seamless connectivity to the Outer Ring Road, Financial District, major IT hubs, international schools, hospitals, and premium retail destinations.

## Overview
Godrej Madison Avenue is a landmark high-rise residential development offering elegantly designed **3 and 4 BHK residences** in one of Hyderabad’s fastest-growing luxury corridors. The project features grand architecture, a **triple-height entrance lobby**, and a **three-level luxury clubhouse** complemented by thoughtfully curated lifestyle amenities. Located in Kokapet, the project enjoys excellent connectivity to the Outer Ring Road, Financial District, and key business hubs. With **RERA approval** and possession scheduled for **December 2029**, Godrej Madison Avenue presents an exceptional opportunity for luxury homebuyers and long-term investors.
