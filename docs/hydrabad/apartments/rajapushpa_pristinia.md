# Rajapushpa Pristinia

## Description
Rajapushpa Pristinia is a premium high-rise residential community by Rajapushpa Properties, located in Kokapet, Hyderabad. Designed to offer modern luxury with serene surroundings, the project features spacious 2 and 3 BHK apartments with contemporary architecture, wide balconies, premium finishes, and abundant natural light.

## Overview
Rajapushpa Pristinia is an under-construction gated community spread across a prime land parcel in Kokapet. The development includes elegant high-rise towers surrounded by landscaped gardens, open green spaces, and pedestrian-friendly pathways that promote both privacy and community living.
Strategically situated near **Financial District, Gachibowli, HITEC City, and Outer Ring Road**, the project ensures seamless connectivity to major IT hubs, international schools, hospitals, and commercial centers — making it ideal for end-users and investors alike.
