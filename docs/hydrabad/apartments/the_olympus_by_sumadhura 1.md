# The Olympus by Sumadhura

## Description
The Olympus by Sumadhura is an ultra-luxury residential apartment project located in Nanakramguda, Hyderabad’s prestigious Financial District. Featuring iconic 44-storey twin towers, the project offers spacious and elegantly designed 3 and 4 BHK residences that combine contemporary architecture, expansive layouts, and world-class amenities for an elevated urban lifestyle.

## Overview
The Olympus by Sumadhura is a landmark residential development spread across approximately **5.06 acres**, comprising **two majestic 44-storey towers** that redefine luxury living in Hyderabad. The project offers meticulously planned homes with expansive layouts ranging from **1,670 to 3,000 sq. ft.**, abundant natural light, large balconies, and breathtaking skyline views. Strategically located in Nanakramguda, the project enjoys seamless connectivity to global tech hubs such as **Amazon, Google, Deloitte, and Wipro**, along with excellent access to major business corridors, making it an ideal choice for discerning homeowners and long-term investors.
