# The Prestige City  


## Description
The Prestige City is a landmark lakefront residential development by Prestige Group, located at Rajendra Nagar, Budvel, in South Hyderabad. Spread across an expansive 64 acres, the project offers thoughtfully designed 2.5, 3, 3.5, and 4 BHK high-rise apartments that combine spacious living, modern architecture, and serene open views. Designed to deliver comfort, privacy, and long-term value, The Prestige City represents premium urban living at a grand scale.


## Overview
The Prestige City is a large-scale premium residential enclave spread across approximately **64 acres**, comprising **13 high-rise towers rising up to 42 floors** and housing **4,647 Vaastu-compliant residences**. Each apartment is designed to maximize natural light, ventilation, and privacy, with select homes offering beautiful lake and open green views. The project features world-class amenities, landscaped greens, clubhouses, and integrated lifestyle conveniences, making it a self-sustained residential destination. Strategically located in Rajendra Nagar / Budvel, the project enjoys excellent connectivity to major city corridors, employment hubs, and social infrastructure in South Hyderabad.
