# Anithara Aura Villas

## Description
Anithara Aura Villas is a premium gated villa community by Sri Hari Hara Estates (Anithara Developers), located in the fast-growing corridor of Isnapur / Patancheru, West Hyderabad. Designed for families seeking privacy, space, and comfort, the project offers beautifully planned 3 BHK duplex villas with private open areas and contemporary architecture.
Spread across a large land parcel, the community provides a serene residential environment away from city congestion while maintaining strong connectivity to key growth corridors. With well-ventilated layouts, quality construction standards, and independent living spaces, Anithara Aura delivers an ideal balance of affordability and comfort.

## Overview
Anithara Aura Villas is an under-construction gated enclave featuring thoughtfully designed 3 BHK duplex villas crafted for modern family living. The project includes landscaped open spaces, wide internal roads, and a secure community layout that enhances privacy and convenience.
Strategically located near **ORR Exit 3**, the development ensures seamless connectivity to Financial District, Gachibowli, HITEC City, and major employment hubs — making it a practical choice for both homebuyers and investors.
