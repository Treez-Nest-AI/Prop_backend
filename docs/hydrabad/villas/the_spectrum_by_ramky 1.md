# The Spectrum by Ramky

## Description
The Spectrum by Ramky is an ultra-luxury gated villa community located in Korremula, East Hyderabad. Featuring elegantly designed 4 BHK triplex villas set amidst lush landscapes and serene open spaces, the project offers expansive interiors, premium specifications, and a refined lifestyle supported by world-class amenities and excellent connectivity.

## Overview
The Spectrum by Ramky is a landmark luxury villa development spread across approximately **13.38 acres**, comprising **151 ultra-luxurious 4 BHK triplex villas** designed for refined and private living. Each villa features spacious layouts with **10-ft ceiling heights**, abundant natural light, private open spaces, and sustainable design elements that enhance everyday comfort and elegance. The community is anchored by a grand **16,830 sq. ft. clubhouse**, complemented by themed parks, landscaped gardens, and thoughtfully curated outdoor recreation zones that create a resort-style living environment. Strategically located in Korremula, the project enjoys excellent connectivity to the **ORR, major IT hubs, leading educational institutions, and key lifestyle destinations**, making it ideal for families seeking serene yet well-connected villa living.
