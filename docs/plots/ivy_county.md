# Ivy County

## Description
Ivy County is a premium villa plotted development located off Gunjur on Varthur–Sarjapur Road, Bangalore. Designed around the natural contours of the land, it offers a tranquil environment with landscaped open spaces and modern infrastructure.

## Overview
Ivy County focuses on nature-inspired living with privacy and long-term value. The project enjoys strong connectivity to major IT hubs, schools, and social infrastructure, making it ideal for families and investors.

## Master Plan / Layout
The master plan preserves natural greenery while providing wide internal roads, community spaces, and well-demarcated villa plots suitable for sustainable living.
